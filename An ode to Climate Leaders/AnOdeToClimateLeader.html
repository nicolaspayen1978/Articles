An ode to climate leaders

By Nicolas Payen | May 26, 2023


To the climate change activists, bold and true,

Your passion ignites, creating a brighter hue.

With banners raised high and voices loud,

You challenge the world and make us proud.


To the politicians, you wield your might,

Crafting policies to set finally things right.

Through negotiations and debates intense,

You strive for change with common sense.


To the corporations, embracing the green,

Your innovations and initiatives, a remarkable scene.

From renewable energy to sustainable production,

You prove that profit and planet can find conjunction.


To the scientists and engineers, you delve deep,

Uncovering secrets that the Earth does keep,

Through research and analysis profound,

You bring knowledge that astounds.


To the Universities, nurturing minds so bright,

Fostering innovation, shedding light,

In lecture halls and laboratories grand,

You shape the leaders who'll take a stand.


Oh, climate warriors, we applaud your might,

For taking up the cause and fighting the good fight.

With humor and determination, you forge ahead,

Inspiring others to join the path you tread.


So let's raise our glasses, toast to your cause,

For working together, we can change the world's flaws.

Keep pushing boundaries, break the status quo,

For a brighter future, let our efforts show!


Cheers to you, climate champions near and far,

You're making a difference, shining like a star!

May your journey be filled with success and glee,

As we strive for a world that's clean and free!
